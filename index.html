DINNER CLASSIC
Classic Herb Roasted Chicken
A succulent roast chicken infused with aromatic garlic, rosemary, and thyme.
timer PREP TIME
20 mins
alarm COOK TIME
1 hr 15 mins
group SERVINGS
4 servings
grocery Ingredients
1 whole chicken(approx. 4 lbs)
4 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
3 sprigs fresh rosemary, chopped
4 sprigs fresh thyme, chopped
4 sprigs fresh thyme, chopped
6 cloves garlic, smashed
1 lemon, halved
Kosher salt and black pepper

Preheat and Prep
Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Remove the chicken from the refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking to bring it to room temperature. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels inside and out.
Season and Stuff
Generously salt and pepper the cavity of the chicken. Stuff with lemon halves and smashed garlic cloves. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine and tuck the wing tips under the body
Apply Herb Butter
In a small bowl, mix the softened butter with chopped rosemary and thyme. Rub the herb butter all over the chicken, including under the skin of the breast for maximum flavor.
Roast to Perfection
Place the chicken in a roasting pan breast-side up. Roast for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and juices run clear.
Rest and Serve
Remove from oven and transfer to a cutting board. Tent loosely with foil and let the chicken rest for 15-20 minutes before carving. This ensures the juices redistribute for a succulent finish.
